How to use Daily Log

    
A pocket coach for whatever you're trying to do every day. Everything lives in your browser.


    
Track a goal

    
    
      
  • Start timer: click the Start button. Card turns red. Only one timer runs at a time, so starting another stops the first.
    • 
      
  • Stop: click Stop. Elapsed time rounds to 5-minute increments and adds to your total for that goal.
    • 
      
  • Set manually: drag the slider, or use the chips:
        
      
          
    • Halfway = half your target
      • 
          
    • Completed = your full target
      • 
          
    • −15 / +15 = adjust by 15 minutes
      • 
        
    
        Skipping a goal? Just leave the slider at 0, or remove the card.
      
    • 
    


    
Manage goals

    
    
      
  • Add: click + Add goal at the bottom of the goal list. Name + target in minutes.
    • 
      
  • Rename: click the goal name. Press Enter to save, Esc to cancel.
    • 
      
  • Remove: hover the card; click the × in the top-right.
    • 
      
  • Reorder: click and drag a card up or down. A green line shows where it'll land.
    • 
    


    
Notes

    
Notes are free-form journaling prompts. Add any label you want: a daily reflection, a gratitude, a blocker, a what-shipped, a what-you-learned. Whatever fits the rhythm you're building.

    
    
      
  • Add: click + Add note. Type a label, press Enter.
    • 
      
  • Rename / remove: same as goals.
    • 
      
  • Empty notes are not written to the saved markdown file.
    • 
    


    
Year heatmap

    
The grid at the bottom shows 12 months at a glance. Each month is its own 5 × 7 block:

    
    
      
  •  Gray — no progress that day
    • 
      
  •  Mid green — some goals hit their target
    • 
      
  •  Bright green — every goal hit its target
    • 
      
  • White outline — today
    • 
    

    
Hover any cell to see the date and how many goals you hit.


    
Save and export

    
    
      
  • Save to file: downloads today's log as a dated markdown file (daily-log-YYYY-MM-DD.md) to your Downloads folder. Click again any time to re-export with the latest values.
    • 
      
  • Copy markdown: copies today's log to clipboard.
    • 
      
  • Preview: shows the markdown that will be saved.
    • 
      
  • Reset day: clears today's sliders and notes. Your goal list and history stay.
    • 
    


    
Privacy

    
Your data lives in this browser only. No cloud, no account, no telemetry. Clearing browser data wipes your local history, so use Save to file daily to keep a portable copy on disk.
